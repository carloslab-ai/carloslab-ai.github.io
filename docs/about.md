# My indie games

## 1. Superbugs Awaken
https://store.steampowered.com/app/1393570/Superbugs_Awaken

<img src="https://cdn.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/steam/apps/1393570/header.jpg?t=1648885106s"></img>

### My team

I've made this game with my friends. Our team has 4 people: 1 designer, 1 artist, 1 marketer and me as an game dev. It's one of the happiest periods in my life. We have numerous unforgettable memories during the journey of developing this game, experiencing both happiness and sadness, but most of the time was filled with happiness. I'm truly grateful for that. I'm also very happy and pround that we were able to finish and release this game.

### Gameplay

It's a Communication-Based, Co-Op game for 2 Players. You and your friend have to choose one of two roles, either as the Scientist or the Hunter. And Mimi, is your precious cat, the 3rd member of your family. Unfortunately, Mi Mi is seriously ill due to being infected by extremly dangerous Superbugs. Therefore, you guys have to collaborate with each other to save Mimi from the damage of deadly Superbugs! 

- The Hunter is a typical teenager and also the son of the Scientist. 
	- His mission is to get inside Mimi's body and fight directly with extremely dangerous Superbugs. 
	- How can he does that? He will be shrunk down to the size of Superbugs (by the shrinking machine invented by the Scientist) and enter Mimi's body through her mouth to help her kill deadly Superbugs.
- The Scientist is scientific genius, the owner of many great breakthrough inventions. His scientific level is at least 50 years ahead of humanity.
	- His mission is to make the right vaccine based on the appearance and the behaviour of Superbugs and send it to his son, the Hunter, to kill these stupid Superbugs.
	- How does he know the appearance of these Superbugs? Yeah, it's through communication. The communication is the only weapon of you guys to save Mimi from deadly Superbugs! 
- Why the communication is the only weapon to save Mimi?
	- The Hunter is inside Mimi's body, so he knows what Superbugs look like and he have to describe the Superbugs's appearance and behaviours to the Scientist.
	- The Scientist is outside Mimi's body, he invented an advanced microscope that could show the location of these Superbugs in Mimi's body but it couldn't clearly show their appearance and behaviours. So he needs to guide his son to the localtion of these Superbugs, make the right vaccine based on his son's description and send it to him to help him kill all the Superbugs before Mimi dies.
