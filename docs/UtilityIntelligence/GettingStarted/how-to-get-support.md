title: How to get support?
---

Currently, there are 3 ways to get support:

1. Join my community and post your questions there: [Join us on Discord](https://discord.gg/vRFEK5uE3f).
	- Firstly, you need to verify your **Invoice Number** to get access to the private support channels.
		- Supporting assets takes a lot of time, and many people **share** assets with their friends, making it **impossible** to support everyone. Sorry if this makes you uncomfortable.
	- This is the **recommended** way to get support because I regularly check messages there.
1. Post your questions on the Unity forum thread: [Utility Intelligence: A user-friendly Utility AI Framework](https://forum.unity.com/threads/utility-intelligence-a-user-friendly-utility-ai-framework.1547540/).
2. Send an email to carloslab.customer@gmail.com, and don't forget to include your **Invoice Number**.
